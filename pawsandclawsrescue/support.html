Donate Now

Supporting our rescue makes a real difference for the animals we care for every single day. Your donation helps cover things like food, medical treatment, comfy bedding, and all the little essentials that keep our pets healthy and safe while they wait for a forever home. Every contribution - big or small - goes straight toward savings lives. It allows us to take in more animals, give them the vet care they need, and make sure they're loved and looked after until the right family come along. When you give, you're not just heping us keep the lights on - you're giving frightened, abandoned, or unwanted pets a second chance. You're helping them feel safe again, helping them heal, and helping them get ready for the moment they meet the person who will change everything for them. If you want to make a real impact in a pet's life, donating is one of the best ways to do it. And honestly, every bit of support truly adds up. Whether it helps us buy an extra bag of food, cover a surprise vet visit, or give a scared animal a soft place to sleep, your generosity makes their world brighter. Together, we can give these pets the fresh start they deserve. Please call or email for more information on how you can contribute today!

Volunteer at Paws & Claws Rescue!

At the Shelter

If you're someone who loves being hands-on and right in the middle of the action, helping out at the shelter is one of the most meaningful ways to get involved. Our animals thrive on interaction, and something as simple as sitting with them, giving them attention, or helping them feel safe can completely turn their day around. Volunteers help us with everyday tasks like cleaning kennels, refreshing bedding, preparing food, walking dogs, or just giving pets the emotional support they desperately need. It's the perfect option if you want to make a direct impact but are'nt able to foster, and every hour you spend truly matters. Even small moments - a quick cuddle, a short walk, or offering a scared pet some reassurance helps them feel loved, confident, and ready for their future home.

Foster

Fostering is one of the most powerful ways you can change an animal's life, and it doesn't take long to see just how meaningful it is. When you take a pet into your home, you're giving them a quiet, safe space to decompress - something that can be hard for them to get in a busy shelter environment. Many of our foster pets are recovering from stressful situations, adjusting to new routines, or simply needing a break from the noise and activity around them. By fostering, you're helping them build trust, learn what it feels to be part of a family, and show their true personalities. We provide everything you need, from food to supplies; you just provide the home, the comfort, and the love. Watching a timid animal come out of their shell or seeing them confidently move on to their forever family is one of the most rewarding feelings you'll ever experience.

Other Ways You Can Help

Can Donation

Another easy and surprisingly impactful way to support our rescue is by donating your aluminum cans. It might feel like something small, but it adds up quicker than you'd expect. Every bag we collect get recycled, and the money earned goes straight toward feeding the dogs and cat who rely on us every single day. These can donations help us stock up on the essentials: quality food, special diets for animals with medical needs, and even the little treats that make their time here more comfortable. It's one of those simple habits that makes a real difference. Instead of tossing can away, saving them becomes a small act of kindness that directly supports the animals waiting for their forever homes. Anyone can help with this, and it's a great way to contribute even if you're on a tight budget.

Tribute Gift

A Tribute Gift is a beautiful way to honor a pet who'll always hold a special place in your heart for only $150. When you purchase a tribute brick, we personalize it with your pet's name and carefully add it to our Walk of Honor - the pathway every adopted pet takes as they leave the shelter and step into their new life. It creates a moment that's honestly really touching. Families walk past names of beloved pets, each brick representing a life that was cherished and never forgotten. Your tribute becomes a part of that story - a permanent reminder of the bond you shared and the love that continues long after they're gone. Beyond the emotional meaning, your Tribute Gift helps us care for the animals currently waiting for their chance. It supports medical care, enrichment, food, and all the little things that make their stay safer and more comforting. It's a way to celebrate your pet's legacy while helping another one move forward into theirs.
